Pizza

Description

Dough, sauce, cheese, maybe some meat or veggies- definitely a comfort food.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1/3 Cup Warm Water

  • 1 & 1/3 Cups Room Temperature Water

  • 2 tsp Active Dry Yeast

  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil

  • 4 Cups Bread or All-Purpose Flour

  • 2 tsp of Salt

  • 2 tsp of Sugar

Toppings

  • Marinara Sauce

  • Cheese

    • Mozarella and cheddar are 2 good options

  • Meat (Optional)

    • Pepperoni, sausage, bacon, etc

  • Veggies (Optional)

    • Mushrooms, peppers, onions, olives, etc

Steps

Dough

  1. Whisk the yeast into 1/3 cup of warm water (around 110°F).

  2. After a few minutes, add the remaining room temperature water and oil and let it sit for 10 minutes.

  3. Separately, combine the flour, salt and sugar in a large bowl.

  4. Pour the yeast mixture into the flour mixture and combine together to create a rough soft ball.

    • Add additional water by the tsp if the dough does not come together into a ball

  5. Scrape the dough out onto a lightly floured surface, and knead the dough for a few minutes. Shape the dough into a ball.

  6. Oil or butter up a large bowl, and place the ball of dough inside. Cover the bowl with a damp clean cloth and leave it somewhere warm to rise for at least 2 hours.

Two Hours Later...

  1. Turn the oven on to 425°F and grab a pizza pan or a baking tray.

  2. Grease the pan or dust it with cornmeal.

  3. Take 1/2 the dough for a big pan, or 1/3 of the dough for a small pan, and carefully stretch it out with your hands

  4. Put the dough in the pan, and carefully stretch the dough to fit.

  5. Cover with a layer of sauce, cheese and your chosen toppings, and bake in the oven for about 15, or until the cheese is nice and melted.

Bon Appetit!
