Pizza

Description

Pizza... you've got dough, sauce, cheese, and the meats.

What else do you need?

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1/3 Cup of Warm Water
  • 1 & 1/3 Cups of Room Temperature Water
  • 2 Teaspoons of Active Dry Yeast
  • 2 Tablespoons of Melted Butter
  • 4 Cups of Bread or All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 Teaspoons of Salt
  • 2 Teaspoons of Sugar

Toppings

  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozarella Cheese
    • You can use other cheese like cheddar or monterey jack
  • Pepperoni, Sausage, or Bacon if desired
  • Mushrooms, Bell Peppers, or Onions if desired
