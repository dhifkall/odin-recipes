Pizza

Description

Pizza... you've got dough, sauce, cheese, and the meats.

What else do you need?

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1/3 Cup of Warm Water
  • 1 & 1/3 Cups of Room Temperature Water
  • 2 Teaspoons of Active Dry Yeast
  • 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 4 Cups of Bread or All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 Teaspoons of Salt
  • 2 Teaspoons of Sugar

Toppings

  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozarella Cheese
    • You can use other cheese like cheddar or monterey jack
  • Pepperoni, Sausage, or Bacon if desired
  • Mushrooms, Bell Peppers, or Onions if desired

Steps

The Dough

  1. Whisk the yeast into the 1/3 cup
    of warm water. Let sit for a few minutes, then
    add the remaining room temperature water and oil.
    Let sit for 10 minutes

  2. Separately, combine the flour, salt and sugar in
    a large bowl.

  3. Pour the yeast mixture into the flour mixture and combine
    together to create a rough soft ball.
    Add additional water (by the teaspoon)
    if the dough does not come together

  4. Throw some flour over a counter or table and Knead the dough
    for a few minutes. Shape the dough into a ball.

  5. Oil (or butter up) a large bowl, and place the dough ball
    inside. Cover the bowl with a damp clean cloth and
    leave it somewhere warm to rise for at least 2 hours.

The Rest

  1. Turn the oven on to 350 F and grab a few metal pans.

  2. Dump the dough out onto a floured surface. Butter up the pan
    or dust with cornmeal and stretch out a 1/4 to 1/3 of the dough
    to fit the pan.

  3. Cover the dough with a layer of sauce, Sprinkle cheese onto the sauce,
    and lastly top with your favorite toppings.

  4. Bake in the oven for 15- 20 minutes, or until cheese is
    nice and melted.

Bon Appetit!
