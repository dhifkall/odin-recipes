Beef Quesadillas

Description

A very simple and easy recipe to make for a busy or lazy night.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 8oz shredded cheese
    • chedder, monterey jack, mexican blend, etc
  • Optional 1 8oz can black beans
  • Optional 2 diced avocados
  • Optional 1 cut up lime, hot sauce and sour cream for serving

Steps

Beef Filling

  1. In a large pan over medium high heat, cook the ground beef until browned.

  2. Add onion to the beef, and cook until the onion is softened (3-4 minutes)

  3. Add the taco seasoning, 1 tablespoon of water and the black beans
    if you have them, and cook on medium low for 5 minutes.

  4. Turn off the heat and add the avocados to the mix.

Assembly

  1. Heat up another large pan over medium heat with 1 teaspoon
    of oil or a good pad of butter.

  2. Lay a tortilla on a plate and sprinkle half with cheese,
    then beef mixture, and then more cheese.

  3. Fold the tortilla in half, and fry on one side until its brown.
    Flip the quesadilla and brown the other side as well.
    • add more butter (if using) after every quesadilla

  4. Repeat with the other tortillas

  5. Cut the quesadilla into wedges, and eat with sour cream,
    lime wedges, and hot sauce if desired.
