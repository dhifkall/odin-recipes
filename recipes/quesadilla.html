Beef Quesadillas

Description

A very simple and easy recipe to make for a busy or lazy night.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 8oz shredded cheese
    • chedder, monterey jack, mexican blend, etc
  • Optional 1 8oz can black beans
  • Optional 2 diced avocados
  • Optional 1 cut up lime and sour cream for serving
