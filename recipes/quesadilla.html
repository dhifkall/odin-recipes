Beef Quesadillas

Description

It's just a tortilla, some meat, and cheese, and you've got a meal! They're great for busy (or lazy) nights.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef

  • 1 Diced Onion

  • 1 Package Taco Seasoning

  • 1 Tbsp Water

  • 4 Large Flour Tortillas

  • 8oz Shredded Cheese

    • Chedder, Monterey Jack, Mexican Blend, etc

  • 1 8oz Can Black Beans (Optional)

  • 2 Diced Avocados (Optional)

  • 1 Lime, Hot Sauce and/or Sour Cream for Serving (Optional)

Steps

Beef Filling

  1. Heat a large pan over medium to medium high heat. Add the ground beef when the pan is hot.

  2. After letting the beef brown up, add the diced onion to the pan and cook until the onion is softened (3-4 minutes)

  3. Add taco seasoning, 1 Tbsp of water and the black beans, if using, and cook on medium low for 5 minutes.

  4. Turn off the heat and add the avocados to the mix.

Finishing it off...

  1. Heat up another large pan over medium heat with 1 tsp of oil or a good pad of butter.

  2. Lay a tortilla on a plate and sprinkle half of it with cheese, then some of the beef mixture, and then more cheese.

  3. Fold the tortilla in half, and fry on one side until it is brown, (2-3 minutes). Flip the quesadilla and brown the other side as well.

    • Add more butter (if using) after every quesadilla, or as needed

  4. Repeat with the other tortillas

  5. Cut the quesadilla into wedges, and eat with sour cream, lime wedges, and/or hot sauce if desired.

enjoy!
